BOOKING ENQUIRIES

  • Availability:

    When you call, we will ask about the date of the event. If Randall is not available on the dates that you want, he will suggest an alternative: remote delivery, webinar, deliver the following year, or suggest another speaker.

  • Fit:

    Randall will only speak at conferences or corporate events where he can do an outstanding job delivering on the objectives of the meeting. He will ask a number of questions about the audience and your objectives, to make sure that his experience and material is a great fit. Of course, he will answer any questions you might have about him and his experience. Download his information package that describes his experience, expertise, and profile in greater detail.

  • Fees:

    Randall practices fee integrity – whether he is booked through a speaker’s bureau or directly, you will pay the same fee. As a good corporate citizen, he does accept a small number of pro bono engagements each year, typically for registered charities where his participation will make a significant difference. If you are working with a tight budget, please do call, as we can be very creative.

  • Extra Value:

    Beyond the usual, we help you market your meeting - and make it more effective. A promo "movie trailer" video, a one page reference sheet, two articles or blog posts, and a webinar of the keynote for those unable to attend. All included.

  • VALUE BEYOND THE EVENT

  • BEFORE

    • A detailed questionnaire about your meeting objectives and audience
    • 2-4 interviews, to understand your priorities and audience issues.
    • Customized and updated presentation.
    • A movie-trailer promo video to help drive registration.
    • (Yes, Randall does rehearse.)

  • ON-SITE

    • Early arrival to meet attendees, listen to other speakers, and weave this intelligence into the presentation. Early arrival also means early sound checks.
    • A powerful presentation that keeps to your schedule.
    • Will stay the day, to spend time with attendees and answer questions personally.
    • Additional learning materials are optionally available (e.g. books).

  • AFTER

    • Two articles or blog posts that can be used either before the event, or to extend the learning afterwards.
    • Randall will present the keynote in webinar format for those who couldn't make the live event, at no additional cost. (We take care of the technology - you just need to send the registration link to potential attendees.)
    • A one-page reference sheet that covers the keynote topic.
